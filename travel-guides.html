Guide to Tokyo

Tokyo is a bustling city where modernity meets tradition. Explore this dynamic metropolis with a 3-day guide to uncover its most fascinating highlights.

Guide to Kyoto

Kyoto is a city of timeless beauty, famous for its temples, shrines, and gardens. Follow this 3-day guide to fully immerse yourself in its rich history.

Guide to Osaka

Osaka offers vibrant nightlife, amazing food, and lively neighborhoods. This 3-day guide will help you dive into the unique charm of this exciting city.

Guide to Hokkaido

Hokkaido is famous for its natural landscapes and outdoor activities. Here is a 4-day itinerary to explore this fascinating island.

Complete Guide to Tokyo

Day 1: Cultural and Traditional Exploration

  1. Senso-ji Temple – Start your day by visiting Tokyo's oldest temple in Asakusa.
  2. Nakamise Market – Enjoy traditional shops selling souvenirs and Japanese sweets.
  3. Ueno Park – Visit the park and its zoo, or relax in the gardens.
  4. Akihabara – If you’re into technology and video games, don’t miss this neighborhood filled with electronics stores and otaku culture.

Day 2: Modernity and Skyscrapers

  1. Shibuya Crossing – Cross the famous intersection and experience the hustle and bustle of the city.
  2. Tokyo Tower – Go up to the observation deck for a panoramic view of the city.
  3. Odaiba – A futuristic shopping and entertainment center on an artificial island.
  4. Shinjuku – Enjoy the skyscrapers, bars, and restaurants in this bustling district.

Day 3: Relaxation and Pop Culture

  1. Meiji Jingu Shrine – Visit this shrine set in a natural environment in the middle of the city.
  2. Harajuku – Explore the district famous for its youthful fashion and unique shops.
  3. Roppongi Hills – Spend the afternoon in this complex of art and shopping, with stunning views.
  4. Robot Restaurant – End the day with a unique show at a restaurant combining robots and pop culture.

Complete Guide to Kyoto

Day 1: Temples and Natural Beauty

  1. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) – Start with one of Japan's most iconic temples.
  2. Ryoan-ji Temple – Admire the famous rock zen garden.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove – Take a stroll through the towering bamboo forest.
  4. Togetsukyo Bridge – Relax by the river at this picturesque bridge.

Day 2: More Temples and Tradition

  1. Fushimi Inari-taisha – Walk through the thousands of red torii gates at this shrine.
  2. Ginkaku-ji (Silver Pavilion) – Relax in the gardens and visit the temple at this UNESCO World Heritage site.
  3. Gion District – Explore the district of geishas.
  4. Minami-za Theatre – If you’re interested in Japanese culture, attend a kabuki performance.

Day 3: Culture and Shopping

  1. Nishiki Market – Sample local food and shop for traditional souvenirs.
  2. Kyoto Imperial Palace – Visit the residence of the imperial family.
  3. Kyoto International Manga Museum – Immerse yourself in the world of Japanese manga.
  4. Shijo Street – Enjoy shopping in this bustling commercial street.

Complete Guide to Osaka

Day 1: Explore the Traditional and the Modern

  1. Osaka Castle – Begin the day with a visit to this iconic castle.
  2. Dotonbori District – Enjoy the neon lights and try famous street food like takoyaki.
  3. Umeda Sky Building – Go up to the observation deck for panoramic views of the city.
  4. Shinsaibashi – Shop in the popular shopping district.

Day 2: Culture and Fun

  1. Shitenno-ji Temple – Visit Japan's oldest temple.
  2. Osaka Museum of History – Learn about the city's history.
  3. Universal Studios Japan – Spend a day at one of the world’s most famous theme parks.
  4. Abeno Harukas – End the day with a visit to Osaka's tallest building and enjoy the views.

Day 3: Food and Entertainment

  1. Kuromon Ichiba Market – Explore this historic food market and enjoy fresh sushi and local delicacies.
  2. Namba Parks – Relax in this urban oasis and enjoy its unique architecture.
  3. Tennoji Zoo – Visit one of Japan’s oldest zoos, located in Tennoji Park.
  4. Sumiyoshi Taisha Shrine – End your trip by visiting one of Japan's oldest and most beautiful shrines.
