Top Things to do

Discover the vibrant streets of Osaka, famous for its restaurants and shops. Enjoy the local culture, try delicacies like takoyaki and okonomiyaki, and marvel at the iconic neon signs illuminating the city. A unique experience to immerse yourself in Osaka's urban life.

Explore the picturesque village of Itomori, famous for its beautiful lake and stunning mountainous landscape. Inspired by the movie "Your Name," stroll through its streets and enjoy the views that served as a backdrop for the story.

Embark on a hike along the Kumano Kodo Trail.This path will take you through dense forests, mountains, and serene landscapes while exploring historic temples and shrines such as the Kumano Hongu Taisha Shrine. The experience offers a deep connection with nature and the spirituality of ancient Japan.
